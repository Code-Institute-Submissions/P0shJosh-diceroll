How to use this site
  • Select your game
  • The top box displays all the available dice
  • Select your dice, and watch as they appear in the next box
  • When you've got your dice pool ready, click "Roll the Dice"
  • The results will appear both below the dice pool, and in your history to the left
  • Keep Rolling! If you need to change the dice pool, just click empty. Simples.
  • Missing the dice you want to play with? Contact me on the "Contact" button!
  • In the settings tab, feel free to turn off the animations and sounds.
